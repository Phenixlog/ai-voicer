# Landing Page Checklist

- Hero: clear value prop, primary CTA, secondary CTA, product preview or mockup.
- Social proof: logos, metrics, testimonials, or case study highlights.
- Problem to solution: short problem framing and how the product solves it.
- Features: 3 to 6 feature cards with crisp titles and outcomes.
- Use cases: 2 to 3 audience or industry blocks.
- Workflow: 3-step how it works section.
- Pricing: tiers, highlighted plan, annual toggle, trust badges.
- FAQ: 5 to 8 questions, concise answers.
- Final CTA: repeated primary action with strong contrast.
- Footer: sitemap, legal, contact, socials, newsletter.
- SEO and accessibility: heading structure, alt text, keyboard focus.
